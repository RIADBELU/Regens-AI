## ✨ Overview

Regen's AI isn't just another chatbot. It's a full platform , with its own AI models, image generation, voice support, a community, and a lot more in the pipeline.

Whether you're a developer, a student, a creative, or just someone who wants a smart assistant on their desktop , this was built with you in mind. Clean interface, fast responses, and no unnecessary fluff.

---

## 🏛️ What It's Built On

### ⚡ Its Own AI Models
Regen's AI runs on models we built ourselves. No relying on third-party APIs for the core experience , just fast, capable AI that we have full control over.

### 🚀 Speed First
Slow AI is frustrating. We've put a lot of work into making sure responses come back fast, so the experience actually feels fluid and natural.

### 🌍 A Real Community
This isn't just software , there's a community behind it. A forum, a blog, people sharing ideas and helping each other out. That side of it matters to us.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 💬 AI Chat
• Clean chat UI with smooth animated message bubbles
• Powered by **Regen's AI 1.0** , our own language model built from the ground up
• Responses stream in real time with a typing animation
• Full markdown support so code, lists, and formatting all look right
• Conversations can be summarized automatically , key points, takeaways, action items

### 🎙️ Voice
• Hold to talk, let go to send , simple push-to-talk input
• AI responses can be played back with a single click
• It just feels natural to use

### 🖼️ Image Generation
• Generate images right inside the chat
• Pick from styles like Cinematic, Ultra-realistic, Retro Neon, Illustration, Anime, and Fantasy
• Powered by **Regen's Nordic 2.0**, our own image model
• Download your images instantly, with optional watermark settings

### 🎬 AI Video Generation
• Turn prompts into short cinematic videos
• Control duration, FPS, and motion smoothing
• Powered by **Regen's Voxel 1.2** *(coming soon)*

### 🌍 Translation
• Support for **52 languages** is on the way
• *(Coming soon)*

### 🔍 Web Search
• Ask anything and get a summarized answer pulled from the web
• Handled entirely by **Regen's AI 1.0** , no external search API needed

### 🔐 Login & Account Security
• Sign in with Email, Phone, Google, Apple *(info website only)*, or continue as a Guest *(info website only)*
• Two-factor authentication with backup codes
• We track login locations and flag anything that looks suspicious , impossible travel, unknown devices, unusual patterns
• You'll get notified if anything seems off

### 🗣️ Community Forum
• A proper forum where users can post, reply, ask questions, and share what they're building
• It's genuinely active and worth checking out

### 📝 Blog
• Users can publish articles, tutorials, and updates right on the platform
• A good place to share what you've learned or built

### 🔔 Notifications
• Email alerts for security events, account activity, and newsletter updates
• Fully customizable , you decide what you want to hear about
